# Post-Exam Pricing and Packaging Plan

Date: 2026-04-29

Scope: SVCollege AI & Full Stack, after the exam-readiness edition is stable.

## Non-Negotiable Rule

All exam-critical learning content stays available in the core portal:

- Lessons
- Concepts
- Question bank
- Mock exams
- Mistake recovery
- D1/D7 learning evidence
- Local progress export

Premium packaging must never replace mastery proof, hide mandatory lessons, hide exam questions, or reduce the ability to study for the exam.

## Packages

| Package | Purpose | Includes | Price |
|---|---|---|---|
| Core Exam Edition | Study for the SVCollege exam | Full lessons, practice, mock exams, retention evidence, local export | unknown/unavailable until real pricing validation |
| Mentor Review | Human review after the learner has real attempts | Code review queue, mentor comments, class handoff report | unknown/unavailable until real pricing validation |
| Teacher Lite | Small class visibility | Class roster, heatmap, risk alerts, Teacher Lite export | unknown/unavailable until real pricing validation |
| Post-Exam Career Practice | Practice after the exam | Capstones, interview drills, portfolio review workflow | unknown/unavailable until real pricing validation |

## Packaging Boundary

Premium can add service, review, exports, cohort management, mentoring, post-exam career practice and operational tooling.

Premium cannot lock:

- A concept required by SVCollege AI & Full Stack
- A level required for Level 100 readiness
- A mock exam question
- A wrong-answer explanation
- A stuck-feedback path
- Any local evidence needed for the learner to understand progress

## Validation Gates Before Charging

Charging stays blocked until all are true:

- `svcollege:readiness:release` passes
- `svcollege:tab-matrix:strict` passes
- `svcollege:command-center:strict` passes
- `metrics:dashboard:strict` passes
- At least one real user or teacher asks for the paid service
- Pricing data is recorded as real sales or explicit willingness-to-pay notes, not invented numbers

## Data Policy

No fabricated revenue assumptions. No invented conversion rates. No projected price points pretending to be validated. Missing business data remains `unknown/unavailable`.
